# Privacy Policy for TuGymFlow

**Last updated:** January 05, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TuGymFlow ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and operate the App:

### Personal Information
- **Name and Email Address:** Collected during account registration, which is typically managed by your Gym Administrator. This information is used for authentication, account identification, and communication related to your account.
- **Profile and Membership Data:** Information related to your role (student, trainer, or administrator) and gym membership status.

### Health & Fitness Data
- **Training Activity:** Exercises performed, training sessions, sets, repetitions, weights lifted, and training history recorded to track progress.
- **Body Metrics:** Optional data such as body weight or other fitness metrics that you voluntarily enter.

Health and fitness data is used **exclusively** to provide training tracking and progress monitoring features.  
This data is **not used for advertising**, **not sold**, and **not shared with third parties outside the core functionality of the service**.

### Device & Usage Data
- **Technical Data:** We may collect anonymized technical information such as application crash reports and performance metrics to improve stability and reliability.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Provide and operate the training tracking service.
- Allow authorized Trainers and Gym Administrators associated with your gym to monitor progress and manage training plans.
- Verify account and gym membership status.
- Improve application performance, reliability, and user experience.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

- **Internal Access:** Your data is accessible only to authorized Gym Administrators and Trainers within your gym.
- **No Advertising Use:** TuGymFlow does not sell personal data and does not use personal or health data for advertising or marketing purposes.
- **Service Providers:** We may use secure cloud infrastructure providers (such as VPS or cloud hosting services) solely to host and operate the application. These providers process data only as necessary to provide infrastructure services and do not use it for their own purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

Your data is retained only for as long as your account remains active or as required to provide the service. Data may be permanently deleted upon account deletion request.

## 5. Data Security

We apply standard security practices, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), authentication mechanisms, and role-based access control. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
