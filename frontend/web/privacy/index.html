Privacy Policy Draft for TuGymFlow

Last updated: January 05, 2026

This privacy policy describes how TuGymFlow ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Name & Email: Collected during account registration (managed by your gym administrator) to authenticate you and personalize your experience.
    • Profile Data: Information related to your membership status.
  • Health & Fitness Data:
    • Training Activity: We record the exercises you complete, weights lifted, sets, and reps to track your progress.
    • Body Metrics: (If applicable) Weight or other metrics you voluntarily log.
  • Device & Usage Data:
    • We may collect app crash logs and performance metrics to improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide the training tracking service.
  • Allow your assigned Professors/Trainers to monitor your progress and adjust specific plans.
  • Verify your gym membership status.

3. Data Sharing

  • Internal: Your data is shared only with the Gym Administrators and Trainers associated with your gym.
  • Third Parties: We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers. We may use standard cloud infrastructure providers (like AWS/Google Cloud via our VPS) to host the service.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures (HTTPS, secure passwords, role-based access) to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
